What is PharmChem CARES?

The Care behind the Service.

The PharmChem CARES is the official academic club of the College of Pharmacy and Chemistry under the University of the Immaculate Conception. The PharmChem CARES stands for Community, Academics, Religious, Environment, and Sociocultural. Each of which are involved in their different activities and projects made by The PharmChem CARES that enhance, promote, and preserve every targeted area.

What are the Areas?

The Five Sides of CARES.

The PharmChem CARES functions as a service-driven organization anchored on five core areas: Community, Academic, Religious, Environment, and Sociocultural. Each of these pillars represents a unique aspect of The PharmChem CARES, showcasing their commitment through various activities, projects, and engagements that highlight, strengthen, and uphold the values of every designated area.
